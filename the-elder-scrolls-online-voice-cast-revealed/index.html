THE ELDER SCROLLS ONLINE VOICE CAST REVEALED

We are pleased to announce that a renowned group of actors will be lending their voices to The Elder Scrolls Online. John Cleese, Michael Gambon, Kate Beckinsale, Malcolm McDowell, Lynda Carter, Bill Nighy, and Alfred Molina – and many more – take on the roles of just some of the interesting characters you’ll meet while exploring Tamriel.

BethBlog: Elder Scrolls Online voice cast revealed

While I’m not exactly especially excited about this game (MMOs makes me (-_-) ), the voice cast unveiled carries some big names and will continue the trend of adding more immersion to the characters in TES. In Oblivion we had Patrick Steward deliver a great performance as Uriel Septim VII, and Skyrim had a great of not well known but great voice actors which made the experience more engaging.

I’ve signed up as a beta tester for this game and while I’m not convinced about it I’ll still give it a whirl as TES fan. I’m not sure if they’ll be NDAs or whatnot, so I may or may not be able to post impressions.
